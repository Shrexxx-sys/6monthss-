Storytimee!!!

met this one girl 9 years ago. same school, same locality, but somehow never really hung out. i had my own group with the boys and there she was, a girl, and i know how that sounds but that’s how it was. we never clicked back then, just existed in the same space without actually knowing each other. then one random day i ended up going to her apartment. no clue why, no plan, just a random instinct. they say god gives you things when you least expect it and maybe that was one of those moments. after that we started playing downstairs, casually, like kids do, but even then something felt different. she was the first person who introduced me to an english song, love me like you do, and it’s insane how that song still means something to both of us, even when we had no idea how much life would change. then i moved out of mumbai, out of state, and time just moved on. we were kids, no phones, no way to stay in touch, and that bond slowly faded like chai left to cool. every time i came back to mumbai i avoided her. not because of anything bad, i was just shy. then out of nowhere, one day i get a follow request on insta. 9 years later. it was her. we started talking again and somehow it didn’t feel awkward at all, it felt familiar. the conversations turned into comfort and one day i asked her out. i wasn’t expecting much, didn’t think she’d say yes, but she did. she actually said yes. and she was everything. sweet, funny, beautiful, kind. the first girl who made me feel genuinely wanted, who looked at all the things i was insecure about and never made me feel small for them. even when i couldn’t explain myself properly she still understood me. and now we’re together.its been 6 months and i couldn’t be happier. it’s long distance, but that doesn’t really matter when it’s her. the same girl who once played love me like you do for me is now the one i love the way she truly deserves.so yes maithili if you're reading this then its true, you are that girl and i love you:)
